Explore a Vast Underwater World

Subnautica allows players to explore a beautiful and diverse underwater world, from shallow coral reefs to deep, dark trenches.

Survive and Thrive

Survival is key in Subnautica. Gather resources, craft equipment, and navigate the dangers of the ocean to thrive in this alien environment.

Encounter Unique Sea Creatures

Meet the fascinating and sometimes terrifying creatures that inhabit the depths. From gentle reefbacks to the aggressive leviathans, Subnautica's marine life is diverse.

Uncover the Mystery

Embark on a captivating storyline as you uncover the mysteries of the planet and strive to find a way to escape. What secrets lie hidden in the ocean depths?

Build and Customize Your Base

Construct your underwater habitat, customize it to your liking, and use it as a safe haven in the vast, unknown ocean.
